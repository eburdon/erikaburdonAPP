About Me

You may have guessed my name by now. Expected to graduate in April 2017 from the University of Victoria in Software Engineering, I'm currently working full time at an industry-changing startup in Vancouver, B.C. I started in computer science by attending various robot and coding events as a young girl. I've since worked internationally in Germany for a research team at one of the leading Universities, and returned to Canada to graduate, adopt a dog, and change the world!

I love sitting on the edge of my skill set. If you visit my Projects page, you can see the latest things I've been working on. In my spare time, I like to rock out to the Killers, flex my skills, or leave it all behind and go camping. I also love really, really bad puns.

If you have a small project or volunteer opportunities for young women and students in Science and Engineering, feel free to get in touch! I'm looking for a range of projects to contribute to - open source or private - and to help encourage women and young students to enter the field.
