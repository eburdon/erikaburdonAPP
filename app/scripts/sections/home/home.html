My name is Erika Burdon, and I'm a 4th year Software Engineering student from the University of Victoria. I'm currently working at an innovative, industry-changing, high-paced startup in Vancouver, B.C. I'm also hard of hearing, so face-to-face or email are my preferred ways to chat.

I really enjoy sitting on the edge of my skill set and taking on new projects. If you're looking for a solution, someone to help you polish off a feature or finish up that odd job, feel free to get in touch! I'm also looking for volunteer opportunities. If you're looking for help with encouraging young students to enter the STEM fields or teaching others about coding and technology, I'd be thrilled to help.

About This Site

This is my full-stack experimental playground. For the front end, I've tried to focus on good design and awesome user interfaces. I've done that by implementing every page completely differently to see what does and doesn't work. The backend / API isn't built yet, but I hope to use Amazon Lambda, a number of different languages, and other neat-o things sometime soon.
